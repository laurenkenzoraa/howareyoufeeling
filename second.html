Impulsiveness:
Impulsivity is the tendency to act without thinking, impulsivity is one of the 
most common symptoms linked to ADHD. This kind of behavior is quite common among 
young people, especially teenagers, but can be more harmful for individuals with 
ADHD.Because of the impulsivity when making decisions it leads to a higher risk or 
injury, substance abuse problems, violence, and disruptive scenarios.
